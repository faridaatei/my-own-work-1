Shinning hopes for communities (shofco)

A place for all

Shinning hope for communities is an organisation located at kibera,it targets the society by offering different programmes,like there is employerbility where the youths are being taught on how to relate with their employer and how they will present theirselves during the interview,also they have young peer providers(ypp)where they educate people on lifeskills concept targetting the younger generation.shinning hope for communities(shofco)also has clinic in that it provides medical health for free without a single cent,they treat all diseases including scanning.the school is mainly for girls and they learn both the 844 and gsce system,they usually go to america when they reach grade 7,those girls are really intelligent.last year they really performed extreamly good and the first 10 girls flew to america and study there while the rest,are being admitted to the most performing schools in kenya.
